## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Technical Risks
- **Scalability Issues**: Implement proper load testing and monitoring
- **Third-Party API Changes**: Build abstraction layers and backup options
- **Security Vulnerabilities**: Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Data Loss**: Comprehensive backup and disaster recovery plans

### Business Risks
- **Competition**: Focus on unique value proposition (AI + trade integration)
- **Market Changes**: Flexible architecture to adapt to new requirements
- **Regulatory Changes**: Stay compliant with data protection regulations
- **Customer Churn**: Implement strong onboarding and customer success programs

### Financial Risks
- **Development Overruns**: Agile development with regular milestone reviews
- **Market Adoption**: Beta testing and iterative improvement based on feedback
- **Cash Flow**: Ensure adequate funding for 24 months of operations
